Best Pharmacology Book

Lippincott Illustrated reviews: Pharmacology

Although there are many good books that cover pharmacology, I prefer Lippincott because it is not too big for a pharmacy student, and it still covers the most importnat pharmacology topics.

Best Community Pharmacy Book

Community Pharmacy Symptoms Diagnosis and Treatment

This book is an indinsible resource for community pharmacists. It explains the most common minor diseases that are encountered by community pharmacists in daily practice. It starts with the diagnosing the disease and eleminating similar conditions. then it provides information on the available OTC treatments.

Best Pharmacotherapy Book

Dipiro's Pharmacotherapy Handbook

This book is short version of the popular Dipiro's textbook. What makes it speacial is it covers pharmacotherapy topics in brief, which helps students remember the most important notes in Pharmacotherapy.
