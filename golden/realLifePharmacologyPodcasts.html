What is Real Life Pharmacology Podcast?

Real Life Pharmacology |Podcast is a free podcast that is produced by meded1o1. Its host is the American pharmacist Eric Christianson, PharmD, BCGP, BCPS.
In each episode, the host chooses one medications and breaks down the most important information regarding the medication. They include mechanism of action, theraputic uses, side effects and drug interactions. By far(June,2024), more than 300 episodes have been produced.

Benefits of Real Life Pharmacology Podcasts

    It is brief
    It provides the listener with accurate information
    It is in audio form, which allows the listener to listen while walking or doing daily activites.
    It can be downloaded and listened to later offline.
    It is completely free.
